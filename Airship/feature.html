Nuke!

A Nuclear explosion that will go off when your ship collides with terrain or a object that would damage a ship. The camera zoom's out to show the nuclear bomb mushroom cloud effect. Nuclear shader was made by Rhys, ship dismemberment by Evan.

Shelf!

A shelving system that shrinks the items in range of the Shelf for easy storage of loads of items but without a need for a UI, this seemless transition is shown in the video below. Each item will shrink to a set amount, or not at all. Made by Rhys
