Airship: Journey To Nowhere

About The Game!

Airship: Journey to Nowhere is a amazing exploration game developed by a small team. It has plenty of cool features such as; procedural terrain from a seed, differant biome's to explore, soothing music that plays as you glide throgh the air and amazing visual effects. The game is still in early development and although it may never leave you can download the most recent version on the download's page or watch the trailer on the right.
